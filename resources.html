Sharing the Ocean tells the story of the grassroots movement we've been working to enrich for the past twenty years. Although Sharing the Ocean tells the story of failure in  fisheries management in the U.S. since the 1976 passage of the Magnuson Fisheries Conservation and Management Act, it also lays the groundwork for hope for the future.
